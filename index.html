  • Pearls of Asia

    6 Days

    Places visited:
    Tashkent - Bukhara - Samarkand - Tashkent fpvierpuerhpgue

    information

    Trip at a Glance:
    Day 1 - Welcome to Uzbekistan
    Day 2 - Bukhorai Sharif, where Emirs come from
    Day 3 - The summer residence of Emirs
    Day 4 - The capital of Temurids
    Day 5 - The place where everything infront of you
    Day 6 - Time to say Goodbye

    Five-day amazing tour through the historical places of Uzbekistan, in Pearls of Asia. You will fell into the atmosphere of ancient cities – Tashkent, Bukhara and Samarkand that sacred the architectural treasure of the hundred years. Every single brick has its own history, every city has its own traditions, and every single family has its own culture. You will visit places enriched with soul of the past and with atmosphere of the present.

    The Samarkand tour program includes a visit of world-famous Registan Square – the center of the capital of the old-time powerful empire founded by Tamerlane, Shakhi-Zinda Complex – one of the sanctuaries of the Islamic world with the shrine of Kusam ibn Abbas, a Prophet Muhammad’s cousin brother, Gur-Emir Mausoleum of the Temurid dynasty and other unique monuments. You will also visit Holy Bukhara.

    Over two thousand years this city, as a mainstay of Islam, has been acquiring its inimitable look. The Samanid Mausoleum, Kalyan Minaret, Ark Citadel and Lyabi-Khauz pond are still cultic structures and symbols of Holy Bukhara up to date.
